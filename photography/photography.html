This short-film is called "窗外的黑羊 | Herd Behavior" which is about the story between reporter and KOL. Nowadays, we can see may public figures have suffered different level of verbal bullying due to private actions. However, the stories written by the reporters are forceful. 2020-2021 Graduation production,
Director Of Photography

This documentary is called "海與岸 | The Hostess".
下海 (go to the sea) is often used to describe female who involve in hospitality industry, falling into an socially unrespectable profession, namely, be drawn into the dangerous sea, which is defined by the society for these mistresses. In the social frame, these mistresses are seen as aphasiac. The Night Club Ladies recorded the authentic face of the three mistresses while working, and how they provoke for destigmatization and organize a union, in terms to communicate with the society. However, the denial and derecognition from the family and their boyfriends turn out to be the biggest challenge. How are they going to face it?

This film is nominated for best 12 of New Taipei City Documentary Film Awards

— Premiere in 2021
